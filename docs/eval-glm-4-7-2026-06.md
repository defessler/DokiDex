- **License MIT** on all three checkpoints (flagship, FP8, Flash) — commercial + derivative + uncensored-finetune OK. No legal blocker for local use or shipping inside DokiDex. **(confirmed)**
- **Context 200K** (`max_position_embeddings` 202752). **(confirmed)**

#### Live risks that map onto YOUR exact config (weight these heavily)

- 🔴 **OPEN bug #21915 — KV-quant gibberish on your exact flags.** With `--cache-type-k q8_0 --cache-type-v q8_0 --cache-reuse 256` (your `coder-fast` lines 24–26), GLM-family models (repro'd on GLM 5.1; a commenter hit it on GLM-4.7-Flash) degrade to gibberish on the 2nd+ message once KV accumulates; not reproducible on Gemma. **Still OPEN.** This is the single most important blocker — your config is the triggering config. **(confirmed)**
- 🟠 **Documented thinking-mode tool-call failure (#19009).** A user reported GLM-4.7-Flash *ignoring* `tool_choice=none/required` and entering an **infinite loop** in `thinking=true + required` mode, while a Qwen-Next MoE handled the same modes correctly. Single-sourced but directly on point for your zero-flake gate. **(confirmed as a user report)**
- 🟡 **Sampling deviates from your defaults.** Unsloth prescribes `--repeat-penalty 1.0`, `--min-p 0.01`, and tool-call `--temp 0.7 --top-p 1.0`; your `coder-fast` block sets none of these and `test-toolcall.ps1` runs temp 0.2. Without these, Flash may loop/underperform — a false-negative risk. **(confirmed)**
- ⚠️ The recon claim that "the FA+quant-KV crash bugs are all closed" **overreaches** — only #19724 (your exact crash combo) and #19169 got real fixes (both predate b9616); the others were stale-bot closures on Vulkan/HIP. The one that matters to you *is* patched in b9616; #21915 (above) is the one that is **not**. **(claim "all closed" → refuted; the load-bearing crash fix → confirmed)**
